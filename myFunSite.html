A Little Bit About Lindsey Stirling

Lindsey Stirling is a violinist, singer, and songwriter.
She was first introduced in season five of America's Got Talent show, where she was quarter finalist. While she did not win, she was able to start her YouTube career.
Each of her videos is unique and she helps choreograph the dances and writes the songs. Her talent has gotten her very far to which she now tours around the world and performing live concerts.
